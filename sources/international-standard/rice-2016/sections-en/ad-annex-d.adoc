== Results of interlaboratory test for husked rice yields

An interlaboratory test <<ref15>> was carried out by the
ENR [Rice Research Centre (Italy)] in accordance with <<ISO5725-1>> and
<<ISO5725-2>>, with the participation of 15 laboratories. Each laboratory
carried out three determinations on four different types of kernel. The
statistical results are shown in <<tableD-1>>.
